This is my 1.1.9 Project! It is Out of this World!

Our project shows a visual representation of the solar system. As you can see from the visual, there is the sun in the center and planet with their orbit paths. Each planet shows the size and color. This is my 1.2.5 Project called Swish!

This is my group's 1.2.5 project. In this project we used gifs to create a basketball game. In this game, there is a pink basketball that moves from side to side on the top of the screen. Once you press a key on your keyboard, the basketball drops. This coding is very similar to our "Apple Avalanche" PLTW lesson. Each time you make the basketball into the basket, the score increases. Your goal is to make the basketball inside the basket each time to get the highest score possible!


This is my 1.3.1 Project called Minion Pong!

This is my group's 1.3.1 PLTW Game Project. In this interactive game, users move minions to play ping pong! The minions act as a paddle as you can move them up and down. In the background, we have Gru from Despicable Me. When the ball hits the wall, the opposing side player wins a point. The first player to reach 7 points wins the game! This game of ping pong allows user movement and also has fun minion characters from the Despicable Me movie!
